About this project

A simple but polished online market .

  • Open the public Render URL (root / shows the UI)
  • Create products (Add page)
  • Read products (Catalog & Admin pages)
  • Update products (Edit page or quick stock update in Admin)
  • Delete products (Catalog/Admin)
  • Explain environment variables: PORT and MONGO_URI

API endpoints

Base: /api/products

  • GET /api/products
  • GET /api/products/:id
  • POST /api/products
  • PUT /api/products/:id
  • DELETE /api/products/:id
